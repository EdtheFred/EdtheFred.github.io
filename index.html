About the Game

Step into the shoes of Detective Spade, a supernatural investigator in a city where shadows come alive. After a mysterious encounter with a shady bartender and a fortuitous rescue of a wizard's cat, you've gained extraordinary abilities that will help you solve the city's darkest mysteries.

Navigate the urban landscape from rooftop to rooftop, using your detective skills and newfound powers to uncover the truth behind the shadowy Wesen creatures that threaten the city.

Game Features

Parkour Movement

Navigate the city's rooftops with fluid movement mechanics. Run from left to right and jump between buildings with precise controls.

Supernatural Powers

Utilize magical abilities including floating and time manipulation. But be careful - these powers are limited and must be used strategically.

Puzzle Combat

Face off against intelligent Wesen creatures by solving letter-based puzzles. One wrong move could be your last.

Noir Atmosphere

Immerse yourself in a dark, mysterious city where every shadow could hide a clue or a threat.
